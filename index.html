About

Doing collections, doing fashion is like a non-stop dialogue. The important thing is to take your time and not get stressed. You have a more interesting life if you wear impressive clothes. Luxury will be always around, no matter what happens in the world.

In order to be irreplaceable one must always be different. For me, the summer will be pure gray - mother-of-pearl gray, very pale gray. To me, this is the big statement for summer. Then we have light blue, light turquoise, lots of pink. I sometimes feel that a pattern is almost a fashion statement in itself. I'm an accomplice to helping women get what they want. Men don't want another man to look at their woman because they don't know how to handle it.

Carl Henk
